Our Story

Meals served with respect, one table at a time

Open Tables – Open Arms began with a simple belief: a warm meal should come with dignity, never judgment. From our food truck, we pull up to the places where help is needed most and set a table for anyone who is hungry.

Founded by [Founder Name], a [veteran / community member] in [City, State], our mission grew out of a desire to meet people where they are — on the street corner, in the shelter parking lot, in neighborhoods the rest of the world drives past. Every plate we serve is a reminder that everyone matters.
